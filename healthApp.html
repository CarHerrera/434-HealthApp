Push Pull Legs

SUMMARY

Push Pull Legs describes a three day split where we group body excercises into three main categories, activieties that involve pushing with your upper body, activities that involve pulling with your upper body and activities that involve mainly legs. To fully utilize this split We recommend cycling them in order and in between allow for one to two rest days, then repeat.
